ABOUT BENUE TECH SKILLS

Benue Tech Skills is a pioneering program initiated by the Benue State government under the visionary leadership of His Excellency Rev. Fr. Dr. Hyacinth I. Alia. The primary goal of this initiative is to empower the people of Benue with in-demand tech skills that are highly sought after globally.

COURSES OFFERED AT BENUE TECH SKILLS

  1. Digital Marketing and E-commerce
  2. Data Science & Analysis
  3. Advanced Data Analytics
  4. UI/UX Design
  5. Front End Development
  6. Backend Development
  7. Cyber Security
  8. IT Support

MODE OF LEARNING

Benue Tech Skills offers flexible learning modes to accommodate different needs: remote, physical, and hybrid. All course materials are available on Google Classroom, allowing learners to access them anytime, anywhere. The physical training is held at the CBT Center in Benue State University, Makurdi. The training commenced on May 27, 2024, and will run for six months.

PARTNERS

This program is supported by esteemed organizations such as Google, Microsoft, and others.

BENEFITS OF THE PROGRAM

Participants in the Benue Tech Skills program enjoy numerous benefits, including:

  • Access to world-class, in-depth training materials curated by leading tech companies like Meta, Google, and Microsoft.
  • Tutoring and mentorship from highly experienced experts.
  • A vast network of co-learners from related fields.
  • Access to well-equipped learning facilities.
  • Job placement opportunities for outstanding trainees upon program completion.
